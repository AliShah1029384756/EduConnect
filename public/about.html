About EduConnect

EduConnect is a passion project built by a solo student developer with a dream — to connect learners, mentors, and counselors on a single platform where knowledge flows freely and support is always close.

This platform was created with dedication, long hours of coding, and a strong belief in the power of education and community. Every feature — from the discussion forum to the counseling section — was designed with a simple goal: to empower students like you.

Whether you're seeking guidance, sharing ideas, or just looking to grow, EduConnect is here for you — built by one, meant for many.

Developed with ❤️ by a solo Computer Science student
